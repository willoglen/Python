Gethsemane DeMolay - The Premier Commandery in MA & RI

Family

Family involvement is a core value, we include our families and friends in many of our social activities, our religious celebrations and provide leadership and financial support to the masonic youth organizations, Rainbow and DeMoly.

Charity

Masonry encourages individual and group charitable actions. Knights Templar have a double obligation as masons and Christians to act to all in a charitable manner. As an individual commandery we contribute to the charitable efforts of the parent organization, the Grand Encampment of Knights Templar USA. They fund research into eye disease and grant scholarships to qualified applicants. At local level we also provide charitable donations to local groups and sponsor scholarships

Christian celebration

Support and engagement in the practice of Christian virtues is a fundamental precept of the organization. We with our family and friends celebrate the core Christian holy days. We observe a Christmas celebration at each commandery, we gather together at Easter to celebrate the culmination of our Saviors life here on earth, we hold services at local churches in celebration together with our community to mark the other significant Christian holy days.

Fraternal Events

Masonry, the world's oldest fraternal organization, has demonstrated the benefits of membership. To each member and their family, friends, and society in general. As Knights Templar we take this to the next level and share Christian fellowship while still practicing the moral and spiritual basics of masonry.
